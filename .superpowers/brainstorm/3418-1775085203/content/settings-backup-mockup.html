How should the overall settings page be organized?

With 4 cards now, the page is getting long. Pick a layout approach:

A

Keep Stacked Cards (current)

4 cards stacked vertically, scroll to see them all. Simple, works fine on mobile. Gets long but each section is always visible.

B

Tab Navigation

Horizontal tabs at the top: General | Theme | Notifications | Backups. One section visible at a time. Cleaner, but hides content behind clicks.

C

Left Sidebar Tabs (Recommended)

Vertical sidebar on the left with section links, content area on the right. Professional settings-page feel (like GitHub/Vercel settings). Best use of widescreen space.
